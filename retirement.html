Retirement Plans

The article reports on an investment to be made by the state government of India in a scheme for providing free electricity to handloom and powerloom weavers in 2007-2008. Report says that 1.35 lakh handloom households and 90,000 powerlooms were covered by the scheme. Also under the scheme, free power up to 100 units would be supplied bimonthly to handloom weavers, who had their own work sheds and were engaged in weaving. The government of India has also increased the old age pension amount for handloom weavers.
